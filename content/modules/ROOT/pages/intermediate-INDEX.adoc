== Overview

These intermediate labs move from knowing defaults to integrating and adapting controls: custom RBAC/SCC, shift-left pipelines, external secrets, audit correlation, compliance tailoring, and admission governance.

Canonical Intermediate skills:

* *I1* — Practical threat modeling
* *I2* — Advanced RBAC and service account hardening
* *I3* — Custom workload security controls (SCC/PSA, SELinux, seccomp)
* *I4* — Tighter isolation / restriction profiles
* *I5* — Advanced network segmentation and egress/ingress controls
* *I6* — CI/CD scanning and policy checks
* *I7* — Secure, reproducible images with signing/attestation
* *I8* — Secret lifecycle (rotation, externalization)
* *I9* — Audit/monitoring correlation
* *I10* — Automated compliance assessment and remediation
* *I11* — Admission and policy controllers

Audience: follow a xref:index.adoc#choose-your-role[role track] for the ordered subset your role owns.
