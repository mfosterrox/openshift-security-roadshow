== Overview

These advanced labs cover enterprise security architecture and zero-trust patterns: governance, GitOps policy, multi-tenant networking, runtime detection, forensics, supply chain integrity, remediation automation, culture, cryptography, and workload identity.

Canonical Advanced skills:

* *A1* — Platform-wide security governance
* *A2* — GitOps-driven policy and compliance pipelines
* *A3* — Multi-tenant zero-trust network and service segmentation
* *A4* — Runtime threat detection pipelines and response integration
* *A5* — Coordinated incident response and forensics
* *A6* — End-to-end supply chain integrity and provenance
* *A7* — Automated remediation and policy enforcement
* *A8* — Security culture and maturity
* *A9* — Advanced cryptography and encryption strategies
* *A10* — Zero-trust workload identity and confidential computing

Application Developer has no Advanced skills in the framework—use Intermediate completion, then DevSecOps or Architect.

Audience and prerequisites:

* OpenShift cluster with cluster-admin (or delegated) access for most labs
* Deep OpenShift networking, security, and architecture familiarity
* Follow a xref:index.adoc#choose-your-role[role track] for the labs your role owns
