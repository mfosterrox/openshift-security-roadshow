== Role overview

Operate, harden, and maintain compliant OpenShift clusters. Enforce day-to-day platform guardrails: access, pod security, networking baselines, quotas, logging, and compliance.

Core responsibilities (framework):

* Configure SCC/pod security, RBAC, and NetworkPolicies
* Node hardening and patch management
* Audit log aggregation and forwarding
* Platform-scoped vulnerability scanning and remediation
* Automate compliance scanning and interpret results
* Security-oriented platform documentation and standards
* Resource quotas, limits, and operational secret rotation
